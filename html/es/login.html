The Room of Beautiful Phonaid

"The Room of Beautiful Phonaid" on YouTube

Play the game with the beautiful Phonaid, the daughter of Phonos the god of sound.

The audio-visual installation "The Room of Beautiful Phonaid" performed on June 18, 2018, as part of the international public-art project "FROM REACHED PUBLIC: HOSPITALITY SESSION".

Mechanics and Installation Algorithm:

Doors to the Room of Phonaid are closed. On the doors there is a text:

The Room of Beautiful Phonaid.

The Rules of the Game:

  1. Follow the numbers.
  2. Remember about the clock.
  3. Try to avoid The Deuce.

The space inside the room is divided into five sectors by lines drawn on the floor. In each sector the numbers from 1 to 6 are written in the form of dots (as on a dice). 2 is missing. Inside of the entrance door there is another sector about 100 x 70 cm size. There is a "START" mark on the border of this sector. Musicians are located around the perimeter of the room. In the center there is a black cube, half of which serves as a DJâ€™s workplace, in the second half located the props of Phonaid: hourglass tray with a dice, and accessories that the actress wears. The mouths of participants in the installation (except musicians) are closed with bandages. The clock on the wall is stopped. The door to the room is locked.

The mistress of the room, Phonaid, goes out to the hall, take a hand of Protagonist and lead the Protagonist into the room. Stops it to the "START" line. Locks the door on her way. Goes to the cube in the center of the room. She takes the hourglass in her hand. She invites the Protagonist into the game with a gesture. As soon as he crosses the line, Phonaid turns the hourglass (3 minutes).
